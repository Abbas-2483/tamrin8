About

"Black Myth: Wukong" is an action role-playing game developed by the Chinese studio Game Science. It is inspired by the classic Chinese novel "Journey to the West," which tells the story of the Monkey King, Sun Wukong. The game has garnered significant attention for its impressive graphics, engaging combat mechanics, and rich storytelling.

Development and Release:

Game Science initially revealed "Black Myth: Wukong" in August 2020 with a highly praised trailer that showcased the game’s mechanics and graphics. Since then, the team has been actively working on development, and the game has generated a significant following due to its impressive previews. As of my last knowledge update in October 2023, there hasn't been an official release date announced, but the game remains highly anticipated among gaming communities.
